About Our Movement

Youth Willers Kanpur is a youth-led initiative creating sustainable change

Founded in 2018, Youth Willers Kanpur has grown from a small group of passionate individuals to a movement of over 50 dedicated volunteers working across multiple social and environmental initiatives.

Our approach combines grassroots activism with innovative solutions to create measurable impact in our community. We believe in empowering young people to become agents of change.
